Poor Nutrition


 Having poor nutrition leads to having bad eating habits. Bad habits can include overeating junk food, starving yourself on purpose, etc. These can lead to serious symptoms such as obesity, diabetes, and can increase risk on strokes, heart diseease, and certain cancers.

Poor Hygiene


 Poor Hygiene has many issues and a number of them can be identified as not brushing your teeth, showering, wearing old clothes, dirty and untrimmed fingernails, and many more. The symptoms can be body lice, athlete's foot, dental caries, and many more, but it can also strongly affect your mental and emotional health.

General healthcare is something that is something that anyone and everyone needs, which makes it so hard to get in the U.S. The effects of poor healthcare can lead to any general problems that people may have with themselves such as limiting one's ability to work, reduce economic oppurtunities, bankruptcy, shorter life expectancies, and many more.
